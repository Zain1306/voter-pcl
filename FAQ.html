Online Voting System FAQ

Q: How do I register to vote online?
A: To register online, visit our registration page and fill out the required information. You will need to provide your Aadhaar number, name, and other necessary details.
Q: Can I vote from anywhere in India?
A: Yes, you can vote from anywhere in India as long as you are a registered voter and have access to our online voting platform.
Q: Is my vote confidential?
A: Yes, we take voter privacy seriously. Your vote is confidential, and we do not share your voting choices with anyone.
Q: How do I reset my password if I forget it?
A: You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Follow the instructions to reset your password.
Q: What do I do if I encounter technical issues while voting?
A: If you face technical issues, please contact our support team at support@example.com or call our helpline at +91-123-4567890 for assistance.
